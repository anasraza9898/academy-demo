How to Apply

The Admission Process

A simple four-step process from first enquiry to joining a class.

1

Submit Enquiry

Fill in the enquiry form on this page or contact us via WhatsApp or phone. Let us know which course and class level you are interested in.

2

Initial Discussion

Our team will contact you to discuss available classes, timings, and any questions you have about the programme and learning environment.

3

Introductory Session

A brief introductory session helps us understand the student's current level and place them in the most suitable class group for their needs.

4

Join Classes

Once admitted, the student begins attending their scheduled class. Progress is monitored from day one and parents receive regular updates.

What to Prepare

Admission Requirements

The requirements below are sample/demo content. Confirm actual requirements directly with the academy.

  • Parent or guardian's contact details

No formal documents need to be submitted online. All requirements will be discussed during your initial conversation with our team.
