The Teaching Team

Teachers Who Are Invested in Every Student

At Sunny Academy, we believe the quality of teaching determines the quality of learning. Our faculty are chosen for their subject knowledge, their patience, and their genuine commitment to helping students understand — not just get through — their coursework.

Teachers at Sunny Academy plan lessons in advance, conduct regular assessments, and make themselves available to students who need clarification. They communicate openly with parents and take individual student needs seriously throughout the academic term.

What Drives Our Teachers

Faculty Values

The shared principles that guide how our teachers approach their work every day.

Thorough Preparation

Lessons are planned in advance so class time is used effectively. Students benefit from structured sessions rather than improvised teaching.

Approachability

Teachers encourage students to ask questions without hesitation. A classroom where students feel safe to ask is a classroom where students actually learn.

Honest Assessment

Progress is communicated clearly and honestly. We celebrate improvement and address weak areas directly so students and parents always know where things stand.

Genuine Care

Every student's progress matters. Our teachers are not simply delivering content — they are investing time and attention in each student's academic growth.
