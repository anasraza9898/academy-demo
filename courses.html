Inside Every Course

Learning Support Included

Every course at Sunny Academy comes with the same core level of teaching support and structure.

Small Group Classes

Every class keeps group sizes manageable so teachers can give proper attention to each student and monitor individual understanding.

Study Notes

Structured notes and topic summaries are provided or guided in class to help students revise more effectively at home.

Topic Tests

Chapter-based tests are conducted regularly to assess understanding and identify areas needing extra work before the next topic begins.

Parent Communication

Parents receive progress updates and can contact teachers directly. We believe keeping families informed leads to better student outcomes.

How We Assess

Assessment Approach

Testing is not just about grades — it is a tool to guide better teaching and more focused study.

1

Topic Tests

Short written tests after each major topic check whether students have understood the material before the class moves forward.

2

Revision Tests

Periodic revision assessments cover multiple topics and help students practise answering under time pressure before exams.

3

Written Feedback

Students receive clear feedback on each test — marking what was done well, and highlighting what needs to be reviewed and practised further.

4

Progress Review

At regular intervals during the term, overall progress is reviewed and communicated to parents so the family can support the student's learning at home.
